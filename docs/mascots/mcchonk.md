## Overview

**Professor McChonk** teaches the Lab how knowledge actually works.

His lectures are informal, his grading opaque, and his conclusions final once he has sat on them.

- Demonstrating that authority can be soft
- Stealing snacks for pedagogical reasons
- Reminding the Lab that certainty is overrated
